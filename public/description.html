B-Cycle Station

A bike share program that provides an alternative to vehicle transportation and reduce carbon emmissions. The clean air and exercise will also improve the health of Charlotte citizens.

Big Belly Station

The Big Belly Station is a solar waste station to further reduce litter, encourage recycling and enable the city to deliver essential services at a reduced cost.

Bike Trail

Bike trails are areas along Charlotte Greenways or in local neighborhoods to ride bikes in a natural setting.

Community Garden

Comminuty Gardens provide green space, improve access to healthy food, shorten the supply chain and provide a recreational activity focused on environmental stewardship.

Electric Vehicle Charging Station

Charlotte's electric vehicle project aims to make transportation more sustainable. Currently there are 26 charging stations in 7 locations around the city.

Farm

Farms provide green space, improve access to healthy food, shorten the supply chain and provide a recreational activity focused on environmental stewardship.

Farmer's Market

Farmer's Markets provide residents and visitors access to locally grown food, promotes local farmers and reduces the carbon footprint of food transportation.

Green Roof

Adding vegetation to the roof tops over parking garages and other buildings that would normally be covered with impervious surface. It improves air quality, mitigates the heat island effect andreduces the amount of runoff that flows into streams.

Greenway/Trail

Greenways aree natural buffers that improve water quality, reduce the impacts of flooding, and provide wildlife habitat. Trails provide recreation, transportation, fitness and economic benefits for all to enjoy.

LEED Certified Building

LEED certification is a rating systems that aims to help owners and operators be environmentally reesponsible and use resources efficiently.

Park

Like the Greenway, Parks are a natural area where residents can enjoy recreational and fitness activities and benefits residents of all ages.

Solar

Photovoltanic energy from the sun is a good energy source in the sunny Charlotte region.

Tree

An effort to replenish and sustain the tree canopy through local collaboration.
